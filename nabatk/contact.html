Still have questions?

Do you deliver outside Cairo? +

Currently, our same-day delivery service is exclusive to Cairo and Giza. We are working on expanding our delivery network to cover Alexandria and other governorates soon.

How long do the flowers stay fresh? +

Our cut flowers are delivered fresh from the farm. With proper care (changing the water every 2 days, trimming stems, and keeping them away from direct heat), they usually last between 5 to 7 days.

Can I schedule a future delivery? +

Yes! During checkout, you can select any date up to 30 days in advance for your delivery. This is perfect for planning birthday or anniversary gifts.
