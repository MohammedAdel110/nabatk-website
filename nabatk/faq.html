Frequently Asked Questions 🌿

Everything you need to know about Nabatk.

🔍

Delivery

Which areas do you deliver to? +

We currently offer delivery across all areas of Cairo and Giza. We are planning to expand to Alexandria and other governorates by late 2026.

How long does delivery take? +

We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM. Orders placed after 2 PM will be delivered the next day. You can also schedule a specific future delivery date during checkout.

Is delivery free? +

Delivery is completely free for all orders inside Cairo. For Giza, a standard delivery fee of EGP 50 applies, unless your order value exceeds EGP 1000.

Can I schedule a specific delivery time? +

While you can select a specific delivery date, we cannot guarantee exact times due to Cairo traffic. Deliveries occur between 10 AM and 8 PM, and our driver will contact you before arriving.

Orders

How do I place an order on Nabatk? +

Browse our shop, choose your favorite plants or flowers, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You don't need an account to place an order, but creating one helps you track it.

Can I modify my order after placing it? +

You can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it. Simply reply to your order confirmation email or contact our customer service.

Is there a minimum order amount? +

No, there is no minimum order amount. You can buy a single small succulent or a massive indoor tree!

Do you offer bulk or corporate orders? +

Yes, we do! Please contact us via the Contact Page for corporate discounts and special bulk event arrangements.

Plants & Care

How do you ensure plant freshness? +

We source our inventory daily from top local nurseries. Plants are kept in our climate-controlled warehouse until dispatch, ensuring they arrive at your door in perfect condition.

What if my plant arrives damaged? +

If your plant gets damaged during transit, please take a photo and send it to our WhatsApp or Email within 24 hours. We will replace it free of charge.

Do you provide care guides with orders? +

Absolutely. Every plant order comes with a printed care card specific to that species, outlining watering, light, and temperature needs.

How are the plants packaged? +

We use eco-friendly kraft paper and secure cardboard boxes tailored to the plant's size. The soil is covered to prevent spilling, and delicate stems are gently wrapped.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 7-day freshness guarantee. If your plant dies or wilts significantly within 7 days of delivery despite proper care, we will replace it or issue a full refund.

How do I request a refund? +

You can request a refund by emailing us your order number and photos of the plant. Refunds to credit cards take 5-10 business days.

Can I exchange a plant for another? +

Yes, as long as the original plant is still in its original healthy condition. A standard pickup and redelivery fee of EGP 50 will apply.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept Visa, Mastercard, Cash on Delivery (COD), Vodafone Cash, and InstaPay.

Is online payment secure? +

Yes, all online payments are processed through our 256-bit encrypted secure gateway partner. Nabatk does not store your credit card details.

Can I pay cash on delivery? +

Yes, Cash on Delivery is available for all orders under EGP 2000. Please try to have the exact change ready.

General

Do you offer gift wrapping? +

Yes! During checkout, you can select the "Gift Wrap" option for an additional EGP 30. We use premium paper, ribbons, and a blank tag.

Can I add a personal message to my order? +

Yes, you can type a personalized message during checkout. We will hand-write it on a beautiful Nabatk greeting card.

Do you have a physical store? +

Currently, Nabatk is an online-only store operating from a secure nursery outside Cairo. This helps us keep costs low and quality high!

Still need help?

Reach out to us directly and we'll get back to you soon.
